The Allnutts Philosophy

Defining the bridge between origin farms and global industry.

Broker, Not Factory

Most exporters sell you what they have in their warehouse. We do the opposite. We operate as a specialized brokerage, representing the buyer's interest. We scan the market to find the processor whose current crop exactly matches your technical specification, regardless of the factory's identity.


The Quality Shield Protocol

In the commodity world, "Grade W320" can mean different things in different countries. Our protocol eliminates this ambiguity. We implement a strict sampling and third-party verification process via SGS or Intertek. We provide you with verified reports before the container leaves the port of origin.


Strategic Origin Advantage

With active operations in Chennai, Kollam, and Đồng Nai (Vietnam), we have eyes on the ground in the world's primary cashew and coffee hubs. This allow us to move faster than the market, securing volumes before pricing spikes.
